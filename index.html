Vesper Studies

A Series of Small Generative Pieces

Short interactive studies in motion, light, and field. Each piece is self-contained and lives at its own page. Hold and release the canvas to play.

No.01

Light Flow Study

Five chapters of flow-field motion. Particles drift, gather, and scatter under a charged-burst gesture.

 → No.02

Pendulum Study

Five chapters of a pendulum-bob field. Hundreds of independent pendulums swing on a jittered grid; hold to gather them, release to scatter.

 → No.03

Sand Study

Five chapters of granular flow. Sand falls from the sky; bend the stream with your cursor, hold to sculpt a pile, tap for a fountain.

 → No.04

Murmuration Study

Five chapters of a boid flock. Hundreds of birds steer by their nearest neighbours; hover to attract, hold to scare, tap to plant a meeting point.
